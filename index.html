Watch, remember, repeat!

The player must repeat random sequences of color by pressing the colored pads in the correct order.

1. After press start, one pad will blink with a sound linked to the pad.
2. After the blink, it's your turn to press the same color. From the second sequence, you need to press all the previous colors and the new color.

e.g. red blinks → you press red → blue blinks → you press red then also blue

good to go
